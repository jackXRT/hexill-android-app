Hexill Privacy Policy

Effective Date: August 18, 2025

At Hexill, we take your privacy seriously—though we also design our platform to operate in a way that ensures security, accountability, and freedom for our company. This Privacy Policy explains, in extensive detail, how we collect, use, store, share, and protect your personal data when you use the Hexill application, website, or services. By accessing or using Hexill in any form, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agreed to the practices outlined here.

1. Information We Collect

Hexill may collect various categories of information, including but not limited to:

  • Account Information: Username, password, email address, profile data, and account preferences.
  • Personal Identifiers: Name, location, phone number (if provided), IP address, device identifiers, and metadata.
  • Usage Data: Browser type, operating system, session duration, clickstream data, pages visited, time spent, and error logs.
  • Transaction Data: Details of purchases, subscriptions, and interactions with payment processors.
  • Blockchain Data: Wallet addresses, transaction hashes, smart contract interactions, and monitoring activity that you voluntarily input.
  • Technical Data: Cookies, beacons, local storage, cache data, and analytics tracking.
  • Communications: Messages, chats, group interactions, feedback, support requests, or reports made through Hexill.

We may collect this information actively (when you provide it) or passively (via automatic systems, cookies, and tracking tools). By continuing to use Hexill, you consent to such collection practices without limitation.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information may be used for the following extensive purposes:

  • To operate and maintain the Hexill app, dashboard, and monitoring services.
  • To provide customer support and respond to inquiries.
  • To improve, customize, and optimize user experience.
  • To track usage trends, analyze system performance, and detect vulnerabilities.
  • To enable features such as wallet monitoring, hash scanning, and contract risk analysis.
  • To enforce our Terms of Service, resolve disputes, and protect Hexill’s rights.
  • To prevent fraud, abuse, hacking attempts, and unauthorized access.
  • To comply with legal requirements, regulatory obligations, and government requests.
  • To generate anonymized data for research, statistics, or business intelligence.
  • To send service updates, promotional emails, alerts, and notifications (which may include marketing).
